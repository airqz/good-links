Dunning Kruger Effect

Inflated self-perception, to think oneself greater than the objective truth. Failure to recognize genuine skill in others.

For the opposite, see Impostor syndrome.

The 7 habits of highly effective artists

Andrew Price tries to become a good artist. He figures out habits which are paramount for quick learning and imporvement.

  • Be specific of what you want to learn
  • Practice actively - No multitasking/doodling
  • Find sources of inspiration
  • Have a loss averse incentive, i.e. take 'damage' if you fail to complete a goal
  • 60% of the time teaches you the most and covers 90% of the work; the last 40% is just perfection and does not produce a lot.
